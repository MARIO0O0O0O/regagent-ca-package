# FORM WVP-FORM-004: VIOLENT INCIDENT LOG
## ⚠️ CONFIDENTIAL — Cal/OSHA Required Record | Retain 5 Years

**Purpose:** Document all workplace violence incidents and threats per SB 553 / Labor Code § 6401.9  
**Access:** Restricted to Plan Administrator, HR, and senior management  
**Availability:** Must be made available to Cal/OSHA upon request  
**Note:** Entries must not identify the victim by name in copies made available to employees

---

### HOW TO USE THIS LOG

**What to log:** Log every incident of workplace violence, every credible threat, and every report of aggressive behavior that causes fear — regardless of whether law enforcement was called or whether injuries occurred. Near-misses are also logged.

**Who logs:** The Plan Administrator logs all incidents. Supervisors may initiate the entry; Plan Administrator completes and reviews.

**Timing:** Log within 24 hours of the incident being reported.
