## üìã RESEARCH TARGET 1: FREE/OPEN-SOURCE TRAINING CONTENT

**Primary Question**: What publicly available harassment prevention training content exists that meets California's strict legal requirements?

### **Specific Content Requirements (CA Law Mandates)**

The content MUST include ALL of the following elements per **Gov. Code ¬ß 12950.1(e)** and **2 CCR ¬ß 11024(d)**:

#### **A. Legal Definitions & Framework**
- Definition of unlawful sexual harassment under FEHA (California Fair Employment and Housing Act) AND Title VII
- FEHA statutory provisions and case law principles
- Types of conduct that constitute sexual harassment (quid pro quo, hostile work environment)
- Remedies available to victims
- Statutes of limitation (1 year for FEHA administrative, 2 years for civil)

#### **B. Prohibited Behaviors**
- Examples of sexual harassment in workplace contexts
- Gender harassment, sexual harassment, and sexually coercive behavior distinctions
- Third-party harassment (customers, vendors, contractors)
- **NEW 2025 REQUIREMENT**: Harassment in virtual/remote work environments and online communications

#### **C. Prevention Strategies**
- Bystander intervention techniques
- How to respond if you witness harassment
- Resources for reporting
- Anti-retaliation protections

#### **D. [POSITION]-Specific Content** (for 2-hour supervisor training)
- [POSITION]'s affirmative obligation to report harassment
- How to respond to complaints
- Conducting preliminary inquiries
- Avoiding retaliation claims

#### **E. Abusive Conduct Training** (SB 1343 Addition)
- Definition of "abusive conduct" (bullying not based on protected characteristics)
- Examples of abusive conduct
- Negative health and economic impacts
- Remedies available

#### **F. Interactive Requirement**
- Training CANNOT be passive (reading materials or watching video alone)
- MUST include question-and-answer component
- Live trainer OR online with interactive features (chat, quiz, live Q&A session)

### **Format Requirements**
- **[POSITION] Training**: 2 hours minimum
- **Non-[POSITION] Training**: 1 hour minimum
- **Delivery Options**: In-person classroom, webinar with live interaction, online with interactive elements

  - A licensed HR professional (SHRM-CP, PHR, IPMA-SCP)?
  - Someone with expertise in employment law?
  - Anyone designated by the employer?

#### **C. Third-Party Training Validation**
- If an employee takes training through Gusto, who validates completion?
- Can HRBiz.org issue a "certificate of compliance" based on third-party training completion?
- Legal distinction between:
  - Certificate of Training Completion (issued by training provider)
  - Certificate of Compliance (issued by employer/HR consultant verifying record)

#### **D. Certificate Content Requirements**
- What MUST appear on a legally valid certificate?
- Required fields per SB 513 (training records mandate):
  - Employee name (full legal name)
  - Training provider name
  - Date & duration
  - Core competencies covered
  - Resulting certification/qualification
- Who must sign the certificate?
- Is a digital signature acceptable?

#### **E. Self-Certification vs. Third-Party Certification**
- Can an employer self-certify that an employee completed training?
- Or must certification come from the training provider?
- What documentation satisfies an audit?
