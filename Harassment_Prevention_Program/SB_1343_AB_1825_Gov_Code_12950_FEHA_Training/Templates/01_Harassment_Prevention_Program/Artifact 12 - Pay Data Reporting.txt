Artifact 12: Pay Transparency, Equity, and CRD Reporting Technical Manual (2026)
Version: 6.0 (Deep Research Audit) Effective Date: January 1, 2026 Primary Mandate: Cal. Lab. Code § 432.3 (SB 642) & Gov. Code § 12999 (SB 464)
1. Pay Scale Disclosure (SB 642)
Legal Authority: Cal. Lab. Code § 432.3 Mandate: > "‘Pay scale’ means a good faith estimate of the salary or hourly wage range that the employer reasonably expects to pay."
A. Posting Compliance
* Disclosure
   * [ ] Include pay scale in every job posting (Employers with 15+ employees).
   * [ ] Third-Parties: Provide scales to job boards (LinkedIn/Indeed); employer remains liable for visibility.
   * [ ] Accuracy: Range must reflect "Day One" expectations, not aspirational career ceilings.
2. Equal Pay Act Expanded "Wages" Audit (SB 642)
Legal Authority: Cal. Lab. Code § 1197.5 Mandate: > "Employer shall not pay any of its employees at wage rates less than the rates paid to employees of another sex for substantially similar work."
A. Compensation Review
* Valuation
   * [ ] Total Compensation: Include bonuses, stock options, travel reimbursements, and gas allowances in equity audits.
   * [ ] Inclusivity: Compare against "another sex" (incorporating non-binary/gender-nonconforming expressions).
3. Annual Pay Data Reporting (CRD) Architecture
Legal Authority: Cal. Gov. Code § 12999 Mandate: > "A private employer that has 100 or more employees... shall submit a pay data report... on or before the second Wednesday of May."
A. Snapshots and Deadlines
* Timeline
   * [ ] May 13, 2026: Submission deadline for 2025 reporting year.
   * [ ] Snapshot: Select one pay period between Oct 1 and Dec 31.
* Labor Contractors
   * [ ] File separate report if 100+ workers hired through labor contractors.
B. SOC Classification Mapping
* 23 SOC Categories
   * [ ] Transition from 10 EEO-1 categories to 23 Standard Occupational Classification (SOC) categories.
   * [ ] Tabulation: Report mean and median hourly rates for every demographic combination within each SOC category.
4. Demographic Data Segregation (SB 464)
Legal Authority: Cal. Gov. Code § 12999(a)(3) Mandate: > "Demographic information gathered... shall be collected and stored separately from employees' personnel records."
A. Firewall Implementation
* Privacy
   * [ ] Digital Partitioning: Ensure HRIS demographic silo is inaccessible to hiring managers.
   * [ ] Sexual Orientation: Collect orientation data on strictly voluntary basis and keep in segregated repository.
