California Leave of Absence Checklist (50-100 Employees)
Version: 5.0 (Deep Research Audit) Effective Date: January 1, 2026 Primary Mandate: FMLA (29 U.S.C. § 2601) & CFRA
1. Federal Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA)
Legal Authority: 29 U.S.C. § 2601 Mandate: > "Eligible employee shall be entitled to a total of 12 workweeks of leave during any 12-month period for... a serious health condition."
A. Eligibility Audit
* Thresholds
   * Headcount
      * [ ] Verify 50 employees within 75-mile radius.
   * Service
      * [ ] Verify 1,250 hours worked in preceding 12 months.
* Concurrency
   * Pregnancy
      * [ ] Explicitly designate FMLA to run concurrently with PDL (State).
   * Bonding
      * [ ] Enforce separation: CFRA Bonding runs after FMLA/PDL expires (Total ~7 months).
   * Workers' Comp
      * [ ] Designate Workers' Comp as FMLA to exhaust federal entitlement.
2. Mandatory FMLA Administration
Legal Authority: 29 C.F.R. § 825.300 Mandate: > "Employer must provide the employee with notice of the employee's eligibility... and a designation notice."
A. Notices & Certifications
* Timelines
   * Eligibility
      * [ ] Issue WH-381 within 5 business days of request.
   * Designation
      * [ ] Issue WH-382 within 5 business days of determination.
   * Certification
      * [ ] Use WH-380-E (Employee) or WH-380-F (Family).
      * [ ] Allow 7-day cure period for incomplete forms.
3. Mandatory Postings (2026)
Legal Authority: Cal. Lab. Code § 1102.8 Mandate: > "Lettering larger than size 14-point type... including the telephone number of the whistleblower hotline."
A. Visual Compliance
* Posters
   * Whistleblower
      * [ ] Ensure font is >14pt.
   * Wage
      * [ ] Post MW-2026 ($16.90/hr).
   * FMLA
      * [ ] Post WHD-1420 (Federal requirement).
4. Record Segregation (SB 513/464)
Legal Authority: Cal. Lab. Code § 1198.5 Mandate: > "Ensure the records include... core competencies... resulting certification."
A. File Management
* Separation
   * Medical (Container 2)
      * [ ] Store FMLA/CFRA medical certs separately from personnel file.
   * Demographics
      * [ ] Segregate race/sex data for Pay Data Reporting (SB 1162).
