California Leave of Absence Checklist (1-49 Employees)
Version: 2.0 Applicability: 1-49 Employees (No FMLA)
1. Mandatory Leaves
* [ ] PDL (1+): Up to 4 months for pregnancy disability.
* [ ] CFRA (5+): 12 weeks for bonding/family care. Consecutive to PDL.
* [ ] Paid Sick Leave: 40 hours/year (SB 616).
* [ ] Bereavement (5+): 5 days.
* [ ] Reproductive Loss (5+): 5 days.
* [ ] Jury Duty / Voting / Crime Victim.
2. Interaction
* PDL + CFRA: Can equal ~7 months total (4mo PDL + 12wk CFRA).
3. CROSS-REFERENCE: HOW OTHER LAWS IMPACT LEAVE
3.1 HARASSMENT PREVENTION → LEAVE
Compliance Impact:
* [ ] Retaliation: Punishing leave-taking is harassment.
* [ ] Deadlines: Extend training deadlines during leave. Cross-Reference: → [Harassment Prevention Checklist]
3.2 PERSONNEL RECORDS → LEAVE
Compliance Impact:
* [ ] Container 2: Store medical certs in Medical File ONLY. Cross-Reference: → [Personnel Records Checklist]
3.3 WAGE & HOUR → LEAVE
Compliance Impact:
* [ ] Sick Pay: Must show balance on wage statement.
* [ ] Final Pay: Accrued vacation must be paid out; Sick leave usually not. Cross-Reference: → [Wage & Hour Checklist]
4. Penalties
* Reinstatement: Court ordered.
* Damages: Lost wages + emotional distress.
