California Pay Transparency & Data Reporting (2026)
Version: 1.0
1. Pay Scale Disclosure (15+ Employees)
* [ ] Job Postings: Must include salary range in ALL postings.
* [ ] 3rd Party: Must provide range to recruiters/Indeed.
2. Pay Data Reporting (100+ Employees)
* [ ] Filing: Annual by May 2nd Wednesday (SB 1162).
* [ ] Content: Race/Sex/Ethnicity by Job Category + Mean/Median rates.
* [ ] SB 464: Segregate demographic data in Container 6.
3. CROSS-REFERENCE
* Harassment: Pay inequity is risk factor.
* Wage & Hour: Accurate hours tracking essential for reporting.
