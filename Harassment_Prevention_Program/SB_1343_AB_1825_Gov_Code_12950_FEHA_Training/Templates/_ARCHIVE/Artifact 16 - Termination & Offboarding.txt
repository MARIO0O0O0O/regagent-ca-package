California Termination & Offboarding Compliance Checklist (2026)
Version: 5.0 (Deep Research Audit) Effective Date: January 1, 2026 Primary Mandate: Cal. Lab. Code §§ 201-203
1. Final Wage Payment and Timing
Legal Authority: Cal. Lab. Code §§ 201-203 Mandate: > "Discharges... wages earned and unpaid... are due and payable immediately... [Quits]... payable not later than 72 hours thereafter, unless the employee has given 72 hours previous notice."
A. Discharge (Involuntary)
* Delivery
   * Timing
      * [ ] Immediate Payout: Deliver check with all wages/vacation immediately at discharge.
   * Remote
      * [ ] Use tracked delivery to ensure receipt by effective termination date.
B. Voluntary Resignation
* Notice Rules
   * 72+ Hours Notice
      * [ ] Pay on last day of work.
   * <72 Hours Notice
      * [ ] Pay within 72 hours of notice.
C. Final Pay Composition (AB 692)
* Prohibitions
   * Deductions
      * [ ] Stay-or-Pay: Do NOT deduct debts for training, relocation, or recruitment (Void Jan 1, 2026).
   * Payouts
      * [ ] Pay all accrued Vacation/PTO. (Sick leave payout not required).
2. Mandatory Post-Separation Notices
Legal Authority: Cal. Unemp. Ins. Code § 1089 Mandate: > "Provide each individual... written notice of his or her unemployment insurance benefit rights by delivering a pamphlet entitled 'For Your Benefit'..."
A. Document Distribution
* Required Forms
   * Change in Relationship
      * [ ] Provide notice stating Name, SSN, and Reason for termination.
   * Benefits
      * [ ] Deliver EDD Pamphlet DE 2320 "For Your Benefit".
      * [ ] Deliver HIPP Notice (DHCS 9061) (20+ employees).
3. Cal-WARN Support & CalFresh (SB 617)
Legal Authority: Cal. Lab. Code § 1401 Mandate: > "Cal-WARN written notices also must include information on whether the employer plans to coordinate services... and provide a description of... CalFresh."
A. Rapid Response
* Disclosures
   * Verbatim Quote
      * [ ] Include: "Local Workforce Development Boards and their partners help laid off workers find new jobs..."
   * Coordination
      * [ ] Provide email/phone of local workforce board.
B. Food Assistance
* CalFresh
   * Info
      * [ ] Include summary of CalFresh, benefits helpline, and website link.
   * Contact
      * [ ] Provide functioning employer email/phone for inquiries.
4. Post-Separation Record Access (SB 513)
Legal Authority: Cal. Lab. Code § 1198.5 Mandate: > "Former employee... has the right to inspect... personnel records... including education and training records."
A. Production
* Compliance
   * Metadata
      * [ ] Produced records must include: Trainer, Date, Competencies, Certifications.
   * Timeline
      * [ ] Fulfill requests within 30 days.
   * Retention
      * [ ] Maintain SB 513 training records for 3 years post-termination.
