California Wage & Hour Compliance Checklist (2026)
Version: 1.0
1. Minimum Wage (2026)
* [ ] State: $16.50/hr (Verify final).
* [ ] Fast Food: $20.00/hr.
* [ ] Healthcare: SB 525 rates.
* [ ] Local (LA): Check LA City/County rates.
* [ ] Exempt Salary: 2x State Min Wage (~$68,640).
2. Breaks
* [ ] Meal: Unpaid 30m before 5th hour.
* [ ] Rest: Paid 10m every 4 hours.
* [ ] Penalty: 1 hr pay for violations.
3. Wage Statements (Lab Code 226)
* [ ] 9 Mandatory Items: Gross, Hours, Rates, Deductions, Net, Dates, Name/ID, Employer Name/Addr, Rates/Hours breakdown.
* [ ] Sick Balance: Must be visible.
4. CROSS-REFERENCE
* Harassment: Training time is paid time.
* Leave: PTO payout rules apply at termination.
* Personnel Records: Keep time records 4 years in Container 4.
