California Cal/OSHA Safety Checklist (2026)
Version: 1.0
1. IIPP (Injury & Illness Prevention)
* [ ] Written Plan: 8 key elements (Responsibility, Compliance, etc.).
* [ ] Access: Available to employees.
2. Heat Illness
* [ ] Outdoor: Water, Shade (>80°F), High Heat procedures (>95°F).
* [ ] Indoor: Triggered at 82°F. Assessment and control measures required.
3. CROSS-REFERENCE
* Workplace Violence: IIPP must integrate WVPP.
* SB 513: Safety training records need metadata.
* Workers' Comp: Injury triggers DWC-1 and investigation.
