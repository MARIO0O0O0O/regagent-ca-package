California I-9 & Immigration Compliance Checklist (2026)
Version: 1.0
1. Form I-9
* [ ] Deadline: Section 1 by Day 1; Section 2 by Day 3.
* [ ] Form: Use 08/01/23 edition.
* [ ] Inspection: Physically inspect docs (unless E-Verify alt procedure).
2. AB 450 (CA Specific)
* [ ] Warrant: Do not allow ICE entry to non-public areas without judicial warrant.
* [ ] Notice: Post notice of inspection within 72 hours of receiving NOI.
3. CROSS-REFERENCE
* SB 294: Notice of rights regarding audits required.
* Personnel Records: Keep I-9s in Container 3 (Separate).
* AB 692: H-1B fees not repayable.
