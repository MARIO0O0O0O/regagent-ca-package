Legal Interaction & Cross-Reference Guide (2026)
Version: 5.0 (Deep Research Audit) Effective Date: January 1, 2026 Purpose: Map legal collisions between mandates.
1. Interaction: Protected Leave & Anti-Retaliation
Legal Authority: Cal. Gov. Code § 12940(h) Mandate: > "Unlawful... to discharge, expel, or otherwise discriminate against any person because the person has opposed any practices... or filed a complaint."
A. Operational Prevention
* Policy
   * Protection Clause
      * [ ] Explicitly state PDL/CFRA/Sick Leave are protected activities in Handbook.
* Deadlines
   * Suspension
      * [ ] Suspend training deadlines (Harassment/Safety) during leave.
   * Grace Period
      * [ ] Grant 30-day grace period for training upon return.
2. Interaction: Leave & Wage Statements
Legal Authority: Cal. Lab. Code § 246 Mandate: > "Furnish... an accurate itemized statement... showing... all applicable hourly rates."
A. Visibility
* Sick Leave
   * Balance
      * [ ] Display available Sick Leave balance on every pay stub.
   * Rate
      * [ ] Calculate sick pay at 2026 minimum ($16.90) or regular rate.
* Termination
   * Payouts
      * [ ] Pay all accrued Vacation/PTO.
      * [ ] Note: Sick leave is NOT paid out (unless rehired within 1 year).
3. Interaction: Stay-or-Pay & Immigration
Legal Authority: Cal. Bus. & Prof. Code § 16608 Mandate: > "‘Penalty, fee, or cost’ includes... reimbursement for immigration or visa-related costs."
A. Visa Fees
* Prohibitions
   * Clauses
      * [ ] Strike H-1B/Visa reimbursement clauses from contracts.
   * Expenses
      * [ ] Categorize visa fees as non-reimbursable employer business expenses (Lab Code 2802).
4. Interaction: New Hire Notices & Workers' Comp
Legal Authority: Cal. Lab. Code § 3550 Mandate: > "Post... notice which shall state the name of the current workers’ compensation insurance carrier... [and] include attorney consultation provisions."
A. Disclosure
* Notices
   * SB 294
      * [ ] Include Workers' Comp rights in the Feb 1 Standalone Notice.
   * Attorney Clause
      * [ ] Ensure poster includes "Right to Consult Attorney" provision.
