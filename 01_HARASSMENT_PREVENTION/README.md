# PACKAGE 01: HARASSMENT PREVENTION
## California AB 1825 / SB 1343 Compliance Package
**Version:** 2.0 | **Last Updated:** February 2026

---

## WHAT'S IN THIS PACKAGE

This package provides everything needed to implement and maintain a legally compliant California Harassment Prevention program for organizations with 5+ employees.

**Training Model:** Live interactive sessions only (in-person or webinar)  
**Employee Training:** 1 hour every 2 years  
**Supervisor Training:** 2 hours every 2 years  
**New Hire Deadline:** Within 6 months of hire or promotion

## KEY COMPLIANCE NOTES

1. **Agency Name:** California Civil Rights Department (CRD) — formerly DFEH, renamed July 1, 2022 (AB 1003)
2. **CRD Contact:** 1-800-884-1684 | www.calcivilrights.ca.gov
3. **CRD Filing Deadline:** 3 years from last incident (increased from 1 year in 2020)
4. **Training format:** Must be interactive — live Q&A required, not passive video
5. **Training records:** Retain minimum 2 years; this package recommends 4 years

## IMPLEMENTATION CHECKLIST

**Initial Deployment:**
- [ ] Adopt policy (HP-POL-001 v2.0) — customize and execute
- [ ] Collect signed acknowledgments from all current employees (HP-FORM-001)
- [ ] Schedule employee training sessions (1 hr)
- [ ] Schedule supervisor training sessions (2 hr, separate session)
- [ ] Begin tracking training deadlines per employee in HP-FORM-003

**Ongoing:**
- [ ] New employees: Collect acknowledgment within 7 days of hire; training within 6 months
- [ ] New supervisors: 2-hr training within 6 months of promotion
- [ ] All employees: Renew every 2 years
- [ ] Complaints: Use HP-FORM-004; investigate within 3 days
- [ ] Policy review: Annually or when law changes
