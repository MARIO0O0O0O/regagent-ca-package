## WHY SUPERVISOR TRAINING IS DIFFERENT

The law requires supervisors to receive **double the training** (2 hours vs. 1 hour) because supervisors carry significantly greater legal responsibility. This training adds:
- Vicarious employer liability â€” what it means for you personally
- Supervisor's duty to report (mandatory, not discretionary)
- Corrective action authority and procedures
- Documentation requirements
- Handling complaints from subordinates
- Managing the team after an investigation
