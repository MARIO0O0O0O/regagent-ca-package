# Government Agency Rights
Employees may file complaints externally with:
• California Civil Rights Department (CRD): calcivilrights.ca.gov, [PHONE]
• U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC): eeoc.gov, 1-[PHONE]
CRD deadline: 3 years
EEOC deadline: 300 days
Most filings are dual-filed based on worksharing agreements.
