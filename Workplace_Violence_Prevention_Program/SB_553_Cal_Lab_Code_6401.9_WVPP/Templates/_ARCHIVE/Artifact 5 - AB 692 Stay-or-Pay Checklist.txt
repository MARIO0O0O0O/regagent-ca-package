California AB 692 "Stay-or-Pay" Prohibition Checklist (2026)
Version: 2.0 Effective Date: January 1, 2026
1. Prohibited Clauses
Void as of Jan 1, 2026.
* [ ] Training Repayment: Clauses requiring repayment for training generally void.
* [ ] Recruiting Fees: Hiring costs.
* [ ] "Quit Fees": Liquidated damages for early resignation.
2. Narrow Exception (Advanced Tuition/Bonuses)
Valid ONLY if ALL 7 met:
1. [ ] Separate Agreement.
2. [ ] 5-day Attorney Consult period.
3. [ ] Actual Cost Only.
4. [ ] Proration (Max 2 years).
5. [ ] No Interest.
6. [ ] Voluntary Resignation Only.
7. [ ] Deferral Option.
3. CROSS-REFERENCE: HOW OTHER LAWS IMPACT AB 692
3.1 HARASSMENT PREVENTION → AB 692
Compliance Impact:
* [ ] Prohibited: Cannot charge/claw back costs for mandatory harassment training. Cross-Reference: → [Harassment Prevention Checklist]
3.2 SB 513 → AB 692
Compliance Impact:
* [ ] Audit: Use training records to identify which trainings are mandatory (non-repayable) vs. optional advanced tuition. Cross-Reference: → [Personnel Records Checklist]
3.3 IMMIGRATION → AB 692
Compliance Impact:
* [ ] Visa Fees: Repayment of H-1B fees is generally illegal/void. Cross-Reference: → [Immigration Checklist]
4. Penalties
* $5,000 per employee: Statutory penalty.
* Attorney's Fees: Recoverable by employee.
