California AB 692 "Stay-or-Pay" Compliance Checklist (2026)
Version: 5.0 (Deep Research Audit) Effective Date: January 1, 2026 Primary Mandate: Cal. Bus. & Prof. Code § 16608 & Cal. Lab. Code § 926
1. General Prohibition on Debt-Triggered Separation
Legal Authority: Cal. Bus. & Prof. Code § 16608(b)(1) Mandate: > "Except as provided... for contracts entered into on or after January 1, 2026, it shall be unlawful to include... a contract term that... Requires the worker to pay an employer... for a debt if the worker's employment... terminates."
A. Identification and Elimination of Void Clauses
* Clawbacks
   * Training
      * [ ] Eliminate TRAPs: Strike clauses requiring repayment for job-related training upon exit.
   * Fees
      * [ ] Recruitment: Remove "replacement hire fees" or headhunter reimbursement clauses.
      * [ ] Immigration: Eliminate repayment requirements for H-1B/visa legal fees.
   * Debt Collection
      * [ ] Prohibit resumption of debt collection (e.g., loans) triggered solely by termination.
2. Exception: Repayment for Transferable Credentials
Legal Authority: Cal. Bus. & Prof. Code § 16608(b)(2)(B) Mandate: > "A contract related to the repayment of the cost of tuition for a transferable credential... [is exempt if] the repayment amount does not exceed the cost to the employer."
A. Credential Verification
* Conditions
   * Transferability
      * [ ] Verify credential is from an accredited third-party institution (e.g., University degree).
      * [ ] Confirm obtaining the credential was not a condition of employment.
* Limits
   * Financial
      * [ ] Cap repayment at Actual Cost (no administrative markup).
      * [ ] Enforce 24-month maximum retention period.
      * [ ] Implement Prorated Repayment (e.g., leave after 1 year = owe 50%).
3. Exception: Discretionary Upfront Payments
Legal Authority: Cal. Bus. & Prof. Code § 16608(b)(2)(C) Mandate: > "A contract for the receipt of a discretionary or unearned monetary payment... [is exempt if] the terms... are set forth in a separate agreement."
A. Procedural Safeguards
* Documentation
   * Standalone
      * [ ] Ensure bonus repayment terms are in a Separate Agreement (not offer letter).
   * Notice
      * [ ] Include 5-day attorney consultation notice.
* Substantive
   * Deferral
      * [ ] Provide written option to defer receipt of bonus until retention period ends.
   * No Interest
      * [ ] Verify repayment carries 0% interest.
4. Enforcement and Penalties
Legal Authority: Cal. Lab. Code § 926 Mandate: > "Liable for actual damages... or five thousand dollars ($5,000) per worker, whichever is greater."
A. Liability
* Damages
   * Exposure
      * [ ] Audit for $5,000 per employee liability for non-compliant contracts.
   * Wage Compliance
      * [ ] Ensure exempt salaries meet $70,304 minimum to prevent misclassification debt claims.
