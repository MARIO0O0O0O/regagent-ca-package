# Protected Categories
The Company provides equal employment opportunity and prohibits discrimination based on all protected categories under:
• FEHA Gov. Code §§ 12940, 12926
• Title VII of the Civil Rights Act

California includes broader protections than federal law including:
Race (inc. traits like protective hairstyles), religion, sex, gender identity/expression, sexual orientation, marital status, medical condition, disability (physical/mental), genetic information, national origin, ancestry, military/veteran status, age (40+), pregnancy/childbirth, reproductive health decision-making, and intersectionality protections.
