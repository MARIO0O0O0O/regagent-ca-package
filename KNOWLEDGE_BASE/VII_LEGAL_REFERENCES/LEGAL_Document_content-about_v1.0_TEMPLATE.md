## What Makes Us Different

### 1. We're Local

We don't serve "California businesses." We serve **Los Angeles businesses**‚Äîand we know the difference matters.

LA has compliance requirements beyond state law:
- Fair Chance Initiative for Hiring
- LA County minimum wage (higher than state)
- Industry-specific ordinances (hospitality, retail)
- Multi-city considerations (different rules in Santa Monica vs. Pasadena)

We track all of it for you.

### 2. We Actually Explain Things

No jargon. No legalese. No "consult your attorney" cop-outs.

When we say "SB 294 requires annual notice distribution," we also explain:
- What a "notice" actually is
- Where to get the official template
- How to distribute it (paper, email, or both?)
- What "acknowledgment" means
- Where to store the signed forms
- What to do if someone refuses to sign

**Our standard:** If a business owner can't implement it after reading our guide, we haven't explained it well enough.

Not "free trial" or "free consultation with purchase." Actual free resources:

**Why?** Because 80% of compliance is information, not implementation. If a template and guide get you compliant, you don't need to pay us. We're okay with that.

For the 20% who need help? We're here with affordable services.
