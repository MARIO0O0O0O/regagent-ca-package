1. 2026 State Wage and Exempt Salary Floors
Legal Authority: Cal. Lab. Code § 1182.12 Mandate: > "The minimum wage for all industries shall be not less than sixteen dollars and ninety cents ($16.90) per hour... exempt... must earn a monthly salary equivalent to no less than two times the state minimum wage."
A. Non-Exempt Compensation
* Hourly Rates
   * Floors
      * [ ] Statewide Floor: Adjust rates to minimum $16.90 per hour (Jan 1, 2026).
      * [ ] Local Ordinances: Audit for "Highest Applicable" local rates (e.g., West Hollywood, Berkeley).
B. Exempt Salary Requirements
* Thresholds
   * White Collar
      * [ ] Annual Floor: Verify minimum $70,304/year ($1,352/week) for Executive/Admin/Professional exemptions.
   * Specialties
      * [ ] Software: Adjust to at least $58.85/hr or $122,573.13/yr.
      * [ ] Physicians: Adjust to at least $107.17/hr.
2. Workplace Know Your Rights Act (SB 294)
Legal Authority: Cal. Lab. Code § 1205 Mandate: > "Beginning February 1, 2026... requires employers to deliver a standalone 'Know Your Rights' notice... covering workers' compensation... immigration... and constitutional rights."
A. Mandatory Notice Distribution
* Timing
   * Deadlines
      * [ ] Feb 1, 2026: Deliver first annual notice to all current employees.
      * [ ] New Hires: Integrate into onboarding packets for hires on/after Jan 1.
* Emergency Contact
   * Designation
      * [ ] March 30, 2026: Provide opportunity to designate contact for arrest/detention.
      * [ ] Protocol: Notify contact if employer has "actual knowledge" of arrest during work hours.
3. Expanded Paid Sick Leave (HWHFA)
Legal Authority: Cal. Lab. Code § 246 (SB 616) Mandate: > "Must allow an employee to use at least five days or 40 hours... Beginning January 1, 2026, accrued California Paid Sick Leave may be used for... court proceedings."
A. Accrual and Payout
* Limits
   * Entitlement
      * [ ] Provide minimum 40 hours or 5 days (whichever greater) annually.
   * Cap
      * [ ] Set accrual cap no lower than 80 hours/10 days.
B. Mandatory Usage Expansion
* Protected Use
   * Judicial
      * [ ] Allow use for Jury Duty or Witness appearances.
   * Victims
      * [ ] Allow use for crime victim judicial proceedings (post-arrest/plea hearings).
4. Workplace Facility & Proximity Benefits
Legal Authority: Cal. Lab. Code § 1031 & 8 CCR § 3396 Mandate: > "Provide access to a sink with running water... in close proximity... [and] drinking water... as close as practicable."
A. Proximity Standards
* Access
   * Lactation/Hygiene
      * [ ] Ensure sink/fridge are within "close proximity" (5-7 min walk).
   * Water
      * [ ] Position drinking water "As close as practicable" (avoid obstacles/excessive travel).
