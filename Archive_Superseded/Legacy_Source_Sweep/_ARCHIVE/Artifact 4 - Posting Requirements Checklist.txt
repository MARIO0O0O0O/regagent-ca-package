California Workplace Posting & Notice Requirements Checklist (2026)
Version: 2.0
1. Mandatory State Posters
* [ ] Minimum Wage (MW-2026): Current rate ($16.50+).
* [ ] Payday Notice: Fillable form.
* [ ] Cal/OSHA: Safety & Health Protection.
* [ ] Workers' Comp: Carrier info & Policy #.
* [ ] Whistleblower: Rights under § 1102.5.
* [ ] Paid Sick Leave: HFA rights.
* [ ] IWC Wage Order: Specific to industry (e.g., Order 4).
2. Federal Posters
* [ ] FLSA: Min Wage.
* [ ] EEOC: Know Your Rights.
* [ ] EPPA: Polygraph.
* [ ] FMLA: Only if 50+ employees.
3. Size-Specific
* [ ] 5+ Employees: Harassment (DFEH-185), Transgender Rights.
4. CROSS-REFERENCE: HOW OTHER LAWS IMPACT POSTINGS
4.1 HARASSMENT PREVENTION → POSTINGS
Compliance Impact:
* [ ] DFEH-185: Must be posted for 5+ employees. Cross-Reference: → [Harassment Prevention Checklist]
4.2 LEAVE LAWS → POSTINGS
Compliance Impact:
* [ ] PDL/CFRA: Required for 5+.
* [ ] FMLA: Only post if 50+. Cross-Reference: → [Leave Checklists]
4.3 WAGE & HOUR → POSTINGS
Compliance Impact:
* [ ] Wage Order: Must post full text of correct industry order. Cross-Reference: → [Wage & Hour Checklist]
4.4 SB 294 → POSTINGS
Compliance Impact:
* [ ] Distinction: SB 294 is a handout, not a poster. Cross-Reference: → [SB 294 Checklist]
5. Penalties
* Civil Penalties: Up to $7,000 per violation (Federal).
* Tolling: Failure to post can pause statute of limitations on claims.
