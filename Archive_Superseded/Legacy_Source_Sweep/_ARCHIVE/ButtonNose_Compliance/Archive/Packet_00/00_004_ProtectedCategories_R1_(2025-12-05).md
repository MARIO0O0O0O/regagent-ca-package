# Protected Categories
This policy protects employees and applicants against unlawful conduct based on all FEHA and Title VII categories:
• Race (including protective hairstyles)
• Color
• Religion/Religious creed (includes dress and grooming)
• Sex (including pregnancy, childbirth, breastfeeding)
• Gender identity/expression
• Sexual orientation
• Marital status
• Medical condition
• Mental and physical disability (CA “limits standard”)
• Genetic information
• National origin and ancestry
• Military/veteran status
• Age 40+
• Reproductive health decisionmaking
• Intersectionality of any protected traits
