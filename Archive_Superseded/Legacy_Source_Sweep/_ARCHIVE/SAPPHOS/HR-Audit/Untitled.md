## THE VIOLATIONS: EVERY FILE HAS AT LEAST ONE

**Medical Privacy (CMIA):** Medical information mixed with general personnel files, stored in unlocked cabinets. Anyone walking by can access confidential health information.

**Immigration (I-9):** Forms scattered across multiple locations instead of consolidated separate file. Cannot produce complete I-9 records if ICE audits.

**New California Laws (SB 464, SB 513):** Took effect January 1, 2026. We're not compliant with either.

**Training Records:** Missing required documentation to defend against harassment or discrimination claims.

**No Security Controls:** No locks, no access restrictions, no audit trails showing who viewed what.

**Root cause:** No HR infrastructure exists. No system, no policies, no trained staff, no procedures.
