# SEI KPI & Chargeability Policies

## 1. KPI Calculation Policy
- Chargeable Hours ÷ (Available Hours - Protected Leave Hours)
- Protected leaves: FMLA, CFRA, PDL, ADA/FEHA, Jury, Military, Voting
- Company leaves excluded: PTO, Holidays, Training

## 2. Equity & Compliance
- Quarterly review of gender, role, and leave patterns
- Disparate impact threshold: >10% deviation

## 3. Reporting & Dashboard
- Weekly timesheet submission required
- HR prepares monthly KPI summary for leadership
