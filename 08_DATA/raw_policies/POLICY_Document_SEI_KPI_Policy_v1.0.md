# SEI KPI & Chargeability Policies

## 1. KPI Calculation
- Formula: Adjusted Chargeability (%) = Chargeable Hours ÷ (Total Available Hours – Protected Leave Hours) × 100
- Non-protected leaves included: PTO, discretionary unpaid leave, partial-day tardies
- Protected leaves (excluded): FMLA, CFRA, PDL, ADA/FEHA, Jury duty, Military leave, Voting leave, PWFA, Bereavement

## 2. Equity & Compliance
- Role-specific utilization bands: Technical 75–85%, Mid-level/PM 75–90%, Leadership 40–75%, Firm average 65–80%
- Quarterly/annual audits for gender, age, disability, and leave patterns
- Disparate impact threshold: >10% deviation requires investigation
- Documentation required for low chargeability justifications

## 3. Operational Guidelines
- Weekly timesheet submission required
- HR reviews leaves and calculates leave-adjusted chargeability
- KPI tables and summaries prepared for executive review
- Annual calibration and legal audit to ensure fairness and compliance
