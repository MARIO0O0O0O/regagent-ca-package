## üéØ HOW THIS SYSTEM WORKS

### The 3-Step Process:

**STEP 1: Download Template**
- Get the base policy template (Word, PDF, or Markdown)
- Review what information you'll need to provide

**STEP 2: Upload to AI + Use Prompt**
- Open your preferred AI (Claude.ai, ChatGPT, Gemini)
- Upload the template document
- Copy/paste the specialized prompt
- Fill in your business details

**STEP 3: Generate & Download**
- AI generates customized policy
- Review for accuracy
- Download as Word/PDF
- Implement in your business

- Review for accuracy
- Ask for revisions: "Make this more specific to restaurants"

**Step 5: Download**

- Save as PDF
