## CONTINGENCY PROTOCOLS

**If Audit Pace Falls Behind Target:**
1. Extend work hours (Mario: +2 hrs/day capacity available)
2. Accelerate Tina to Level 3 access earlier (parallel audit support)
3. Prioritize high-risk employees (terminated, recent hires, positions requiring licensure)
4. Request timeline extension with Controller approval

**If Critical Violations Require Immediate Action:**
1. Pause systematic audit to remediate (e.g., I-9 >10 days overdue)
2. Engage legal counsel for interpretation
3. Document remediation actions in audit trail
4. Resume systematic audit once critical risk mitigated

**If System Technical Failure:**
1. Activate daily backup (encrypted external drive)
2. Restore from last known good state (48-hour recovery target)
3. Document incident in audit log
4. Implement additional redundancy measures
