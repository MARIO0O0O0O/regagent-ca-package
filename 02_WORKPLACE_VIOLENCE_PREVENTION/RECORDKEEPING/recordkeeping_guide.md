## 1. WHAT RECORDS MUST BE KEPT

### A. Training Records (Annual)

| Record | Content | Retention |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Training sign-in roster (WVP-FORM-002) | Names, dates, trainer, duration | 5 years |
| Training completion log (WVP-FORM-003) | Master record all employees annually | 5 years |
| Training acknowledgments (WVP-FORM-001) | Signed per employee per year | 5 years |
| Training content / materials | Agenda, handouts, WVPP summary used | 5 years |
| Trainer qualifications | Name, title, basis for qualification | Duration of service + 5 years |

> **SB 553 Requirement:** Records must show that ALL employees were trained, not just a portion of the workforce.

### B. Violent Incident Log (WVP-FORM-004)

The violent incident log is the most critical SB 553 record. It must:
- Document every workplace violence incident
- Document every credible threat
- Document every report of aggressive behavior causing fear
- Be maintained even if no incidents occurred (zero-incident periods should be noted)
- **Retain for 5 years**
- Be made available to Cal/OSHA upon request
- Be available to employees upon request — **BUT names of victims must be removed** from copies provided to non-management employees

### C. WVPP Document Itself

| Record | Retention |
|--------|-----------|
| Current WVPP | Active |
| All prior WVPP versions | 5 years from version date |
| Annual review records (WVP-FORM-005) | 5 years |
| Hazard assessment documentation | 5 years |

### D. Cal/OSHA Reporting

| Situation | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Work-related fatality | Report to Cal/OSHA within **8 hours** — 1-800-963-9424 |
| Serious injury or illness | Report to Cal/OSHA within **8 hours** |
| Inpatient hospitalization (1+ employees) | Report to Cal/OSHA within **8 hours** |
| OSHA 300 recordable injuries | Log within 7 calendar days |

**Cal/OSHA 24-hour hotline:** 1-800-963-9424  
**Online reporting:** www.dir.ca.gov/dosh

## 2. WHERE RECORDS ARE STORED

### Filing Structure
```
WVP COMPLIANCE FILES
├── WVPP_DOCUMENTS/
│   ├── Current WVPP (active)
│   └── Prior Versions (by date)
│
├── TRAINING/
│   ├── Sign-In Rosters (by date)
│   ├── Training Logs (WVP-FORM-003 master)
│   └── Acknowledgments (by employee)
│
└── INCIDENTS/ (RESTRICTED ACCESS)
    ├── Violent Incident Log (WVP-FORM-004 — cumulative)
    ├── [Case #] — [Date] — Investigation File
    └── Annual Review Records (WVP-FORM-005)
```

### Access Controls
- **WVPP and training records:** Plan Administrator and HR
- **Incident log:** Plan Administrator, HR Director, and senior management only
- **Copies to employees:** Provide with victim names removed

---

## 3. EMPLOYEE ACCESS TO RECORDS

Under SB 553, employees have the right to request a copy of the violent incident log. When providing copies:
- Remove names of victims
- Retain all other incident information
- Log the request and fulfill within a reasonable time
- Do not charge employees for copies

---

## 4. CAL/OSHA INSPECTION READINESS

If Cal/OSHA conducts an inspection related to workplace violence:

**Immediately make available:**
- [ ] Current written WVPP
- [ ] Violent incident log (last 5 years)
- [ ] Training records for all current employees
- [ ] Annual review documentation
- [ ] Hazard assessment records

**Do not:**
- Alter or destroy any records once inspection is initiated
- Provide unredacted victim information to non-authorized parties
- Refuse to cooperate — Cal/OSHA has inspection authority

**Do:**
- Notify legal counsel as soon as inspection begins
- Document what was provided and when
- Request a copy of any citation

---

## 5. ANNUAL COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST

Complete at each annual WVPP review:

**WVPP Document:**
- [ ] Current WVPP in effect and dated
- [ ] All prior versions retained with dates
- [ ] Annual review completed (WVP-FORM-005)

**Training:**
- [ ] All employees trained in current year
- [ ] Training records complete and organized
- [ ] No employees overdue for annual training

**Incident Log:**
- [ ] All incidents logged within 24 hours of report
- [ ] Log entries complete (no blank fields)
- [ ] All corrective actions documented
- [ ] Log retained for 5 years

**Cal/OSHA Reporting:**
- [ ] All reportable incidents reported within required timeframe
- [ ] OSHA 300 log current
